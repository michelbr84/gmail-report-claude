### How to Create Filters in Gmail

1. Go to **Gmail → Settings (⚙) → See all settings → Filters and Blocked Addresses**
2. Click **Create a new filter**
3. Enter the criteria from the table above in the **From** or **Has the words** field
4. Click **Create filter**
5. Check **Apply the label** and select or create the suggested label
6. Optionally check **Also apply filter to matching conversations**
